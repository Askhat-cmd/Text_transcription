# PRD-046.0.2 Chunking Quality Comparison

## Baseline (PRD-046.0.1)
Observed on synthetic practice_manual:
- `theory=3`
- no reliable section boundary typing (`practice/lens/safety` collapsed into theory)
- governance classification happened post-chunking with limited structural context

## After PRD-046.0.2
Observed on synthetic practice_manual:
- `lens=1`
- `practice=1`
- `safety=1`
- `theory=0` for this fixture

## What Changed Technically
- Added deterministic section parser with markdown/chapter/numbered heading recognition.
- Added boundary metadata to `UniversalBlock`:
  - `heading_path`, `section_role_hint`, `boundary_confidence`, `split_reason`, `parent_section_id`
- Upgraded `BookChunker` to role-aware section splitting with practice-step preservation.
- Governance adapter now prioritizes structural role hints.
- Chunking quality trace now includes boundary and mixed-intent diagnostics.
- Chroma/query pass-through now exposes safe quality summary fields.

## Net Effect
Quality moved from token-only chunk boundary behavior to structure-aware governance-ready chunks, which is required before safe runtime filtering and later Diagnostic Center layers.
