Включает Enum для уровней и обратной связи, Request Models (AskQuestionRequest, FeedbackRequest, GetUserHistoryRequest, GetStatsRequest) и Response Models (SourceResponse, StateAnalysisResponse, PathRecommendationResponse, AnswerResponse, AdaptiveAnswerResponse, etc.).
# Details:
Файл: bot_psychologist/api/models.py

Содержимое:
1. ENUMS: UserLevel (beginner/intermediate/advanced), FeedbackType (positive/negative/neutral)
2. REQUEST MODELS:
   - AskQuestionRequest: query, user_id, user_level, include_path, include_feedback_prompt, debug
   - FeedbackRequest: user_id, turn_index, feedback, rating, comment
   - GetUserHistoryRequest: user_id, last_n_turns
   - GetStatsRequest: user_id (optional), time_range_days
3. RESPONSE MODELS:
   - SourceResponse: block_id, title, youtube_link, start, end, block_type, complexity_score
   - StateAnalysisResponse: primary_state, confidence, emotional_tone, recommendations
   - PathStepResponse: step_number, title, duration_weeks, practices, key_concepts
   - PathRecommendationResponse: current_state, target_state, key_focus, steps_count, total_duration_weeks, first_step
   - AnswerResponse: status, answer, concepts, sources, metadata, timestamp, processing_time_seconds
   - AdaptiveAnswerResponse: всё от AnswerResponse + state_analysis, path_recommendation, feedback_prompt, conversation_context
   - ConversationTurnResponse, UserHistoryResponse, FeedbackResponse, HealthCheckResponse, ErrorResponse, StatsResponse
